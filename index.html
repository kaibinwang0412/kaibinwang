About Me

I am Kaibin Wang, a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computing Technologies, Swinburne University of Technology, under the supervison of Prof. Qiang He and Prof. Yun Yang. My current research interests include Federated Learning (FL), Edge Computing, and FL+LLM. I publish in top journals and conferences, such as EuroSys, WWW, and TSC. More details are in here.



I am currently seeking a postdoctoral position in the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Large Language Models (LLM), Federated Learning, and Edge Computing. I am eager to contribute to cutting-edge research that explores innovative applications and methodologies in these areas. With a strong academic background and hands-on experience in machine learning, AI systems, and distributed computing, I am enthusiastic about collaborating with experts to advance the frontiers of AI research, particularly in the context of real-world applications like privacy-preserving AI and efficient, scalable computing solutions.
